About me:

This is my website named "An Escape".
We always have many moments in our life where we want to just get rid of our current situation and we want a better life though most of the times we do envy others but there are times when everything in your life is a mess, there are problems and lots of problems whether in family, life, friend circle, loved ones, partner, etc, there are always some sort of problems and we want to escape from that situation for a while. Want to take a break from everything? This is what my sites's name says...
I do write poetries, short stories and also listen to all your problems as a good buddy. I also have a blog having the same name, you can go and check out the ongoing story of a small town girl Shamya who is finding her own agenda of life.
